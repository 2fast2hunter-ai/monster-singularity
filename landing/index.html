Core Loop

An ecosystem that never stops — even when you do.

AETERNUM is an offline-capable idle game. Your monsters generate energy while you sleep, work, or live your life. Return to find your farm evolved, your resources banked — or your ecosystem on the edge of collapse.

1
Farm energy offline

Every monster generates resource ticks around the clock. Log in to collect, reinvest, and expand your stable.

2
Enter the genetic editor

Splice gene sequences from two parent species. Compatible sequences produce new offspring. Incompatible sequences have consequences.

3
Unlock new species

Successful breeds unlock entries across 10,000+ species. Each creature belongs to one of five stability classes — from docile to catastrophic.

4
Manage the singularity arc

The late game unfolds in real time, measured in months and years. Your choices now determine what your ecosystem becomes.

⚠ Ecosystem warning Miss 48 hours and your ecosystem starts to destabilize. Unstable monsters don't wait.
