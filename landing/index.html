What Makes It Different

Breed + Genetics

Every species carries a unique gene sequence. Cross two parents and watch their DNA combine — or catastrophically destabilize. Mastering the genetic system is the key to discovering the rarest monsters in the game.

The Omni-Dex holds over 10,000 distinct monster species, each with its own rarity, stability class, and production rate. No two playthroughs look the same. The catalog is the endgame.
