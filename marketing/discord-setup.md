### Channel Structure

#### INFORMATION (read-only)
| Channel | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `#announcements` | Official game updates, patch notes, alpha milestones. @everyone pings here only. |
| `#roadmap` | Pinned document: current alpha priorities + upcoming features. Updated each sprint. |
| `#rules` | Community guidelines. One post, pinned. |

#### COMMUNITY
| Channel | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `#general` | Open chat. Game talk, memes, introductions. |
| `#introductions` | New players say hi and share their starter species. |
| `#off-topic` | Anything not game-related. |

#### GAMEPLAY
| Channel | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `#breeding-labs` | Breeding strategies, gene sequence theory, combo discoveries. Main strategy channel. |
| `#omni-dex-finds` | Share rare species discoveries, Omni-Dex completionist progress. |
| `#auction-board` | Weekly auction discussion, bid strategy, auction results. |
| `#farm-showcase` | Screenshots of your collection and production stats. |

#### FEEDBACK
| Channel | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `#bug-reports` | Structured bug reports. Pinned template: OS, browser, steps to repro, expected vs actual. |
| `#feature-requests` | Upvote-style suggestions. Use Forum channel type with voting enabled. |
| `#alpha-feedback` | General alpha impressions and balance feedback. |

#### TEAM
| Channel | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `#dev-log` | CTO/dev posts raw notes, build updates, and behind-the-scenes progress. Informal. |
