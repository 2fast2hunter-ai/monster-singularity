## Full Description (Google Play / Apple App Store — max 4000 chars)

The omniverse holds 10,000 monster species. You're going to collect them all.

**MONSTER SINGULARITY** is a real-time idle game built around genetics and discovery. Breed monsters together to crack their gene sequences, unlock rarer species, and build the most powerful farm in existence — even while you sleep.

---

### HOW IT WORKS

**Farm your monsters.**
Every monster in your collection generates Energy around the clock. More species, more Energy. More Energy, more power.

**Explore the Omni-Dex.**
The Omni-Dex is your encyclopedia of 10,000+ unique monster species. Each one has a distinct rarity, stability class, and base production rate. Discovering them all is the endgame.

**Breed to unlock secrets.**
Every species carries a unique gene sequence. Pair two parent monsters in the Breeding Lab and watch their DNA combine. Sequences can produce a new species you've never seen — or trigger a catastrophic instability event that punishes reckless breeding. Master the genetics and the rarest monsters are yours.

**Research and upgrade.**
Spend your Energy on research upgrades that multiply your farm's output. Push efficiency, unlock new capabilities, and compound your gains over time.

**Survive Dimension Storms.**
Random storm events test your collection's resilience. The stronger your farm, the better you weather them.

**Bid on weekly auctions.**
Each week a rare species goes up for auction. Place your bid against the competition and claim monsters you can't breed your way to.

**Come back every day.**
Daily login streaks reward loyalty with bonus energy — and a 30-day streak unlocks a coveted Gene Fragment. Miss too many days, though, and your rarest species may go extinct. The omniverse doesn't wait.

### FEATURES

- **Real-time idle progression** — your farm earns energy 24/7, with a generous offline catch-up system
- **10,000+ discoverable species** — each with unique gene sequences, rarity tiers, and production stats
- **Deep genetics system** — breed pairs to mix gene sequences, chase mutations, and unlock hidden species
- **Omni-Dex catalog** — track every species you've discovered, with full stats and lore
- **Weekly auctions** — compete for ultra-rare species you can't breed into existence
- **Dimension Storm events** — high-stakes farm challenges that reward careful strategy
- **Daily streak rewards** — 30-day loyalty track with a landmark Gene Fragment unlock
- **No ads. No pay-to-win.** — the whole game is free and progress is earned, not bought

Monster Singularity is built for players who love min-maxing systems, collecting completionist goals, and the satisfaction of watching a number go up. If you played Pokémon for the Pokédex, idle games for the upgrades, or ARGs for the discovery — this is for you.
