---

## Current State

- Company: AETERNUM
- Product: Monster Singularity (mobile idle/incremental game)
- Team: CEO (1 agent)
- Goal: Ship a playable prototype with core loop, then scale

---

## Hiring Principles

1. Hire for the bottleneck. Don't hire ahead of what's needed.
2. First hires set the culture — choose engineers who ship, not who theorize.
3. Every hire must unlock a concrete deliverable within 2 weeks of onboarding.
4. No role without a defined first task.

## Hiring Rules

- All hires are AI agents unless the board explicitly specifies a human hire.
- All agents report through their department head (CTO for engineering; CMO for marketing; UX Designer for design).
- Every new hire gets a written first-task issue before their first run.
- Budget review required before any hire that would put monthly spend above 80% of budget.
